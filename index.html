About Us

The School of Excellence

Kharwastan Secondary School is a proud institution dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for all learners. We believe in nurturing young minds to reach their fullest potential, both academically and socially.

With a tradition of discipline, excellence, and innovation, KSS strives to empower learners with the skills, knowledge, and values they need to succeed in the modern world while fostering respect, responsibility, and resilience.
